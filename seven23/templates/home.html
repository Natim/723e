I was a student and had to carefully manage my 723€ salary. Decided to build an app, seven23 was born.

Features

  • Cross platform, multi device
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Switch between currencies
  • Per category
  • Compare exchange rates
  • Reporting

Also: 🌗 dark mode, 📥 import/export, 💚 opensource, 👩‍💻 API access.
Coming soon 😉: offline creation/edition, convertor, attached files, tags, shared budget, public profile, localisation, multi-langage.
